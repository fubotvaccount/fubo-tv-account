How to Login to Your Fubo TV Account
====================================

Follow these simple steps to sign in:

**Step 1**

Open your preferred web browser or launch the Fubo TV app.

**Step 2**

Visit the official Fubo TV login page.

**Step 3**

Enter the email address associated with your account.

**Step 4**

Enter your account password.

**Step 5**

Click or tap the **Log In** button.

**Step 6**

Complete any required verification if prompted.

**Step 7**

Once signed in, start streaming your favorite live TV channels and on-demand content.

Login Using the Fubo TV Mobile App
==================================

You can also sign in through the Fubo TV app.

* Open the Fubo TV app.
* Tap **Log In**.
* Enter your registered email address.
* Enter your password.
* Tap **Log In** to access your account.

Login on Smart TV or Streaming Device
=====================================

To sign in on a supported TV or streaming device:

* Open the Fubo TV app.
* Select **Log In**.
* Enter your account credentials or follow the on-screen activation instructions if provided.
* Start watching live TV and on-demand entertainment.

Common Fubo TV Login Issues
===========================

Forgot Password
---------------

Use the password reset option on the login page to create a new password.

Incorrect Login Credentials
---------------------------

Check that your email address and password are entered correctly.

Subscription Issues
-------------------

Verify that your Fubo TV subscription is active if you cannot access premium content.

App Not Working
---------------

Restart the application and update it to the latest available version.

Internet Connection Problems
----------------------------

Ensure your device has a stable internet connection before signing in.

Browser Cache Issues
--------------------

Clear your browser's cache and cookies if login problems continue.

Security Tips
=============

* Create a strong and unique password.
* Never share your login credentials.
* Sign out after using public or shared devices.
* Keep your account information updated.
* Install the latest version of the Fubo TV app.
* Monitor your account activity regularly.

Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I log into Fubo TV on multiple devices?**

Yes. Fubo TV supports streaming on multiple compatible devices, depending on your subscription plan.

**How do I reset my Fubo TV password?**

Click the **Forgot Password** link on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your registered email.

**Is Fubo TV login secure?**

Yes. Fubo TV uses secure authentication to help protect your account and personal information.

**Can I use the Fubo TV app on my smartphone?**

Yes. Official Fubo TV apps are available for Android and iPhone devices.

**Why can't I log into my Fubo TV account?**

Common reasons include incorrect login credentials, password issues, inactive subscriptions, browser problems, outdated apps, or internet connectivity issues.
